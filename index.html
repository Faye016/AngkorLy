Koh Rong in Cambodia is a tropical paradise known for its stunning white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush jungles. Highlights include Long Beach, glowing bioluminescent plankton at night, and vibrant coral reefs for snorkeling. It's a mix of backpacker vibes and luxury resorts, with opportunities for jungle hikes, island hopping, and cultural experiences in local fishing villages. Known for graceful **Classical Khmer Dance**, flavorful dishes like **Amok**, and vibrant celebrations like **Khmer New Year**, it emphasizes family, respect, and tradition.
